Jon Snedden, the photographer who shot Sally Spencer for page 14 of this issue, was born in Fife, Scotland, and studied photography at the University for the Creative Arts. He currently lives in London and works all over the UK shooting for editorial magazines, advertising agencies and charities. His clients include Ikea, Tesco, Macmillan and the British Heart Foundation. He says: “It was great fun to shoot Sally in such a fabulous new office space, more media agency-looking than accountancy firm. It created a great, friendly atmosphere.”
