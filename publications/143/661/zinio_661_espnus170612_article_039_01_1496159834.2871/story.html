“WHAT HE DOES TOUCHES THE IMPOSSIBLE. WE’RE USED TO IT, BUT IT IS A DELIGHT AND A PRIVILEGE TO HAVE THE BEST PLAYER IN THE WORLD IN YOUR TEAM.” BARCELONA MANAGER LUIS ENRIQUE ON MESSI

He remains the greatest current exponent of soccer, a player of such otherworldly skill that the one remaining question in his career is whether he is the best in history. We could wax lyrical all day about the Barcelona and Argentina nonpareil’s unreal goals, visionary assists and magical left foot, but it’s better to follow the advice of his old mentor Pep Guardiola: “Don’t write about him, don’t try to describe him. Just watch him.”
