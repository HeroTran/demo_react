—JIM CAPLE

 

Nadal is the undisputed King of Clay, with a record nine championships at Roland Garros to go with five other grand slam singles titles. Nothing if not consistent, he won at least one slam in 10 consecutive years (2005-14) and has surpassed $80 million in career earnings. If that’s not enough, there’s an asteroid named after him: 128036 Rafaelnadal. (No planet is named after a tennis player yet; Venus doesn’t count.)
