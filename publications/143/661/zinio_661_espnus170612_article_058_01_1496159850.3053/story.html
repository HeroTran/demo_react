—THOMAS NEUMANN

 

Twelve years ago, Brady was pictured snuggling a goat in a GQ photo spread. Now, after winning his fifth Super Bowl, there’s little doubt that Brady is the GOAT. If you want hard stats, Brady has them, ranking among the NFL’s top five passers in attempts, completions, yards and touchdowns. His sights will be on another record as he turns 40 in August: oldest QB to win a Super Bowl.
