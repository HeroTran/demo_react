Standing just 4-foot-8, Biles is often referred to as the Michael Jordan of gymnastics. She won four golds in Rio—a record for an American female gymnast in a single Games (and twice as many as His Airness!). The byproduct of her success? She became a megastar, appearing on Dancing With the Stars and being named a finalist for Time’s Person of the Year, an honor that eventually went to then-President-elect Donald Trump. Biles 2032!
