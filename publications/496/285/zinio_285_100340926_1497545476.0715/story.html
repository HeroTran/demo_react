NIKE DAY TO NIGHT APPLE WATCH BANDS

nike.com

Apple’s collaboration with Nike has already produced a popular, breathable band for the Apple Watch, and now that same band style is available in several different colors. The $49 Day to Night collection is made up of bands that are, “inspired by a shade of the sky,” according to Nike. The four bands are available in colors that match the four colorways of the Air VaporMax Flyknit running shoe, in various shades of blue and purple. While Nike says that the new bands are for Apple Watch Nike+, there’s nothing about the new Day to Night bands that add functionality to the watch—you can pick one up regardless of the model of your Apple Watch.—ROMAN LOYOLA

WATERFIELD DESIGNS iPAD PRO GEAR CASE

sfbags.com

Now that there’s a new iPad Pro on the market, you might find yourself with more gear to schlep around than usual. Enter WaterField Designs’ $49 iPad Pro Gear Case, a simple waxed canvas and leather pouch with specialized pockets to stash all of your iPad goodies. It has room for out-of-the-box necessities, like your Lightning cable, USB power adapter, and EarPods, as well as space for extras like the Apple Pencil or a battery pack. Its mesh pockets will ensure that nothing gets tangled or lost, and the Gear Case can be easily stored in another purse or laptop bag.—LEAH YAMSHON

MY MIGGO PICTAR FOR iPHONE 7

mymiggo.com

The $99 Pictar camera lens kit transforms your iPhone into a DSLR-style camera, offering a shutter-button grip that includes “half press” mode to lock focus and “full press” for immediate shutter release. Its companion iOS app adds even more functionality, featuring 10 pre-set modes to maximize shooting efficiency. Better yet: If you’re photographing in the cold, Pictar makes it easy to shoot pictures while wearing gloves.—JOEL MATHIS
