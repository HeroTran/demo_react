Charge Running

Charge Running (free, iPhone and Apple Watch) puts a different spin on the usual running apps. It offers three live runs a day—8:00 a.m., noon, and 6:00 p.m.—and lets you join with other runners across the country, pitting your progress against theirs and competing on a live leaderboard.

Joyable

Joyable (free, iPhone) promises to help you reduce anxiety through the use of cognitive behavioral therapy. Download and take a quiz about yourself, then you’ll receive assigned five-minute activities to help you through the process of learning to control your more extreme emotions. You’ll work with a coach to set goals and measure progress.

Prizmo Go

Prizmo Go (free, in-app purchases, iPhone and iPad) lets you cut and paste real-world text using your device’s camera: Just shoot, and then you can highlight text, copy it, or tap to call phone numbers, send email, or open an address in Apple Maps.
