Unlike high-cost, mass-market prosthetics, 3D-printed hands can be quickly repaired and have custom designs and features such as NFC modules in the fingertips, which could do everything from unlocking smart locks to authenticating mobile payments. Exiii engineers are testing an infrared muscle sensor to control the limb. It’s buggy, but it’s cheap and simple to set up.
